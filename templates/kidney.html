Kidney Disease Prediction Model

Kidney Disease is a chronic medical condition that impacts the proper functioning of the kidneys, vital organs responsible for filtering waste and excess fluids from the blood. It can manifest in various types, including chronic kidney disease (CKD), acute kidney injury (AKI), and polycystic kidney disease (PKD).Early detection and management of kidney disease are vital to slow its progression and prevent further damage. Symptoms may include fatigue, swelling in the ankles or hands, changes in urine output, and high blood pressure. Regular monitoring, lifestyle modifications, and proper medical treatment can help mitigate complications and improve overall kidney health, reducing the risk of kidney failure and related issues like cardiovascular problems and bone disease.
